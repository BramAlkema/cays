Integrating Capabilities.

CAYS is a Capability Integrator that designs, builds, and operationalises mission-critical capabilities for defence, security, and strategic public missions, together with industry, government, and investors.

Modern defence and security challenges no longer require individual systems, they require integrated, deployable capabilities. CAYS exists to bridge the gap between operational needs, industrial innovation, and long-term investment, ensuring that critical capabilities move from concept to reality at speed.

What We Do

Capability Integration

CAYS does not manufacture products.
We integrate capabilities by orchestrating the ecosystem around a clearly defined operational need.

Our Core Activities

  • Translate operational needs into capability roadmaps
  • Define common architectures, interfaces, and governance
  • Organise competitive mini-competitions within the ecosystem
  • Integrate, verify, and validate the delivered capability
